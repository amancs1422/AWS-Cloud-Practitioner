# Setting up a Zero Spend Budget
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Author%20-%20Aman%20Kumar-blue) ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Tags%20-%20AWS%20Budgets-yellow)
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/AWS%20Billing%20and%20Cost%20Management-orange)

### Step 1: Login to AWS Management Console and search for Billing and Cost Management.

![Billing_Login](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Zero_Spend_Budget%201.png?raw=true)

### Step 2: Find Budgets and click on it.

![Budgets_click](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Zero_Spend_Budget%202.png?raw=true)

### Step 3: Click on “Create a Budget” button.

![Create_budget_button](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Zero_Spend_Budget%203.png?raw=true)

### Step 4: Choose the following options as shown in the image below. We are setting up a budget so we get notified if/when we incur our first $.

![Budget_Options](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Zero_Spend_Budget%204.png?raw=true)

### Step 5: Give the budget a name and provide an email address so we get an email if we incur our first cost on the AWS account.

![Enter_Email_Address](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Zero_Spend_Budget%205.png?raw=true)

### Step 6: Congratulations!!! You just configured your first Budget in AWS.

![Congartulations](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Zero_Spend_Budget%206.png?raw=true)
