## :rocket::rocket:Launching an EC2 Instance

### Step 1: Log into your AWS management console, search for EC2 and click on it.
![Search_EC2](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Launch_EC2_Instance%201.png?raw=true) 

### Step 2: Click on Launch instances button here and provide a name to your instance.
![Launch_Instance_Button](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Launch_EC2_Instance%202.png?raw=true) 

### Step 3: For now, we will leave the rest of settings as it is(default) and click on Launch instance button.
![Instance_Settings](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Launch_EC2_Instance%203.png?raw=true)

### You should see a message like this.
![Successful_launch](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Launch_EC2_Instance%204.png?raw=true) 

### Step 4: Go back to instances section and you should see your newly launched instance present there.
![Instance_List](https://github.com/amancs1422/AWS-Cloud-Practitioner/blob/main/Images/Launch_EC2_Instance%205.png?raw=true)
